A scandal in Putney

To Sherlock she was always always known as 'her'. For 'him' a reference with any
greater clarity was an unwanted intrusion into the deeper workings of his own mind.
A mind that ran like a Turing Engine. A misstep would break the very logic that
gave him the certainty that allowed him to described the world around him in
such exquisite detail.

Whenever the human condition became the focus of his steely attention, it was always
most cutting. No layer of information was left unturned.
